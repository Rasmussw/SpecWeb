2. Our intention

The ClimbAlong Services are made for everybody who loves sportsclimbing. ClimbAlong consists of various types of climbing-related apps that support climbing-related activities. The Services include a competition management platform, an app for individual climbers and other types of apps.

Accordingly, a very integrated part of providing the Services is to collect and distribute information about those who use the Services including name, age, gender, nationality, as well as routes, climbs, competitions, results, rankings, etc.

We will always do our utmost to protect and safeguard your information to avoid any unwanted or criminal use of your data.

Please note, that if you do not want to share the types of information about yourself mentioned above via ClimbAlong, then you should either not provide it, or refrain from using ClimbAlong. If you do not want to use your real name, in most instances it is possible to use an alias instead.

Our intention is to make it easier:

  • For climbers to track performance, find good climbs, arrange small competitions, share their progress and results, engage with other climbers – in short to be a better climber, engage with other climbers, and to have more fun climbing,
  • For event organizers (clubs, federations, and others) to plan competitions or other types of events, highlight and promote their competitions/events, show the results and rankings of those who participate in competitions / events, engage climbers and audiences, attract sponsors etc.

The use of a pixel tag allows us to record, for example, that you have visited a particular web page or clicked on a particular advertisement. We may also include web beacons in e-mails to understand whether messages have been opened, acted on, or forwarded.

Operationally Necessary.

Performance-Related.

Functionality-Related.

We may use Technologies that allow us to offer you enhanced functionality when accessing or using our Services. This may include identifying you when you sign into our Services or keeping track of your specified preferences, interests, or past items viewed.
